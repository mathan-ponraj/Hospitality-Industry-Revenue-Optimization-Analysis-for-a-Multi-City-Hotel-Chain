# Hospitality Revenue Optimization Analysis

## Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution designed for Atliq Hotels, a multi-city hotel chain. I developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to solve the problem of declining revenue and low occupancy rates. By moving away from manual reporting to a data-driven approach, this tool helps management identify underperforming properties and optimize pricing strategies.

## The Business Problem
Atliq Hotels faced inconsistent occupancy across different cities and lacked clear insights into why certain properties were underperforming. The goal was to analyze booking patterns, identify revenue leakage (from cancellations and no-shows), and understand the profitability of various booking platforms like Online Travel Agencies (OTAs).

## My Technical Workflow

1. Data Modeling (Star Schema): I structured the data using a Star Schema to ensure high performance. I separated fact tables (bookings and revenue) from dimension tables (cities, dates, and room categories), making the data easy to query and report.
2. DAX Measure Development: I authored over 25 custom DAX formulas to track critical hospitality KPIs such as RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room), ADR (Average Daily Rate), and Occupancy %.
3. Interactive Dashboarding: I designed a multi-level dashboard that allows stakeholders to filter data by City, Property, or Booking Platform to find specific areas for improvement.
4. Revenue Leakage Analysis: I built specific views to quantify losses from cancellations and "no-shows," providing a clear picture of missed revenue opportunities.

## Key Business Insights
- Demand Trends: Weekend demand is significantly higher than weekdays, suggesting a need for dynamic pricing or weekday-specific promotions.
- Channel Profitability: While OTAs bring in high volume, the commission costs impact the bottom line, highlighting the need for more direct bookings.
- Occupancy Imbalance: Certain cities showed consistently lower occupancy, pinpointing exactly where marketing efforts should be focused.

## Tools and Technologies
- Business Intelligence: Power BI
- Data Transformation: Power Query
- Analytical Logic: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Architecture: Star Schema Data Modeling

## Suggested Business Impact
- Potential to increase profit rates by up to 30% through data-driven dynamic pricing.
- Capability to reduce cancellation rates by 25% by implementing better booking policies based on trend data.
